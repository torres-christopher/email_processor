FAQ

Email Monitor is a web-based email management and organization tool designed to simplify and improve the email experience for users. It offers features for categorizing and sorting emails by brand and category, making it easier to access and manage email communications.

The primary goal of Email Monitor is to enhance email management efficiency and productivity by providing a user-friendly platform for categorizing, sorting, and accessing emails based on brands and categories.

Email Monitor was created to inspire CRM professionals by sharing various companies email communication. Traditional email clients are limited to single people use, hence direct marketing. Email Monitor was developed to offer a solution to these issues.

Yes, you can contribute to the Email Monitor project. We welcome contributions from the open-source community. If you're interested in contributing, please refer to our GitHub repository for more information on how to get involved.

You can contact the Email Monitor project team by sending an email to k.torres@seznam.cz.

Email Monitor utilizes a variety of technologies, including Flask for the web framework, SQLite for the database, APScheduler for scheduling, and various Python libraries such as email, Pillow, and requests to handle email processing and rendering.

Yes, Email Monitor is an open-source project. You can find our project repository on GitHub, where you can access the source code and contribute to its development.
